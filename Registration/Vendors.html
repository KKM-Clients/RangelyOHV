Rangely OHV Adventure Rally August 9-11, 2018
Sponsor & Vendor Package

Be a vendor at the OHV EXPO! The expo will be held during the event at the Rangely Automotive Museum, basecamp for the Rally. The Expo is open for registered riders and the public.

Pricing is for inside or outside. Inside space is a first request basis.

Vendor Levels

  • Level 1: $50, 10’ x 10’ space, 1/8 page ad in Trail Guide Booklet
  • Level 2: $200, 15’ x 20’ space, 1/4 page ad in Trail Guide Booklet
  • Level 3: $300, 15’ x 20’ space, 1/2 page ad in Trail Guide Booklet
  • Level 4: $500, 15’ x 20’ space, 1/2 page ad in Trail Guide Booklet, trail sponsorship, four rider registrations

Contact us for semi-truck or custom size space requirements. Vendor payments may be all cash or equivalent product donation Every vendor level will include:

  • Two rider registrations, all other riders get a discounted rate of $50
  • 2 event t-shirts
  • Business logo and link on website, logo on posters, Trail Guide Booklet and t-shirts. See due dates for logos below

Sponsor Levels

(for businesses not attending as a vendor)

Level 1: $500 or more monetary or donated product value

Level 2: Up to $500 monetary or donated product value

  • Sponsor levels include business logo and link on website, logo on posters, Trail Guide booklet and t-shirts. See due dates for logos below
  • Donated product values are based on retail
  • Donated product will be raffled off at the event
